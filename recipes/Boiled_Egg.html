Fried Egg:

A boiled egg is a superb dish that is known for it's healthiness and yumminess. We will be making a good soft boiled egg!

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Small pot
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Turn the heat for the Pot
  2. Add enough water to fully submerge egg
  3. Put the egg inside without cracking
  4. Now let it stay on medium heat for 8 minutes
  5. The water will be boiling and once 8 minutes has passed, take out the egg
  6. Turn off the heat
  7. Let the egg cool a bit and then unpeel it
  8. Now add a pinch of Salt and Black papper
  9. That's it! a delicious and very easy to make dish!
