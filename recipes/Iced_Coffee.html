Fried Egg:

Summers are very hot and a drink like this will make sure to keep you fresh.

Ingredients:

  • Instant Coffee
  • Cup
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Milk
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Add a tiny amount of water to the cup and heat it in microwave
  2. Add 1 and half spoon of sugar and a spoon of instant coffee to the heated cup
  3. Use a spoon to grind and mix the sugar and coffee in the hot water until it's fluffy brown
  4. Now add the cold milk and mix it again using the spoon
  5. Add some ice
  6. That's it you have some delicious Iced Coffee
